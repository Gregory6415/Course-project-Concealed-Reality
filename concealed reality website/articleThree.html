The end

It's time to break the silence and we owe it to everyone who got involved in our journey. It's with a huge weight that we're announcing that Concealed Reality is now over.

Unfortunately, and with a bitter taste, we will not perform a farewell show, neither the booked shows.

There's no need to go deeper about the reasons we are all closing this chapter of our life.

Nonetheless, we want to thanks everyone who came to a show, streamed/bought our music over the world, danced in pit, screamed our lyrics and wore our merchandising.

There will be a last sales on our very last pieces of merchandising to keep remaining our music online on every actual social platforms.
Sincerely.
